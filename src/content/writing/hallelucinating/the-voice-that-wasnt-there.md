My Bloody Valentine, 1988. Vocalist Bilinda Butcher often mentions how she would intentionally be made to record her vocals right after she had woken up, during the making of album Loveless. This provided a sleepy quality to the vocals that would beautifully complement the hazy and blissed out soundscapes that guitarist Kevin Shields created, seemingly out of thin air.
