So I wanted to announce a new series of posts called 'This Album Changed My Life'. Aspiring writers/music enthusiasts who want to share a writeup about an album that influenced them, why it holds such a significance and a brief overview of the lyrical themes etc. are welcome to contribute. Anupama will be writing the first article in the upcoming series... Expect new things soon, and follow the page for more! (Pictured is my favorite album of all time, Elliott Smith by Elliott Smith. A writeup on this is ongoing, but tough to do justice to because of the sheer power of this record, and the often brilliant lyrics and lyrical themes. It should be up in a while nonetheless)
