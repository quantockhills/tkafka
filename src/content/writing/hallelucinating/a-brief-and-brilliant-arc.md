'Long afloat on shipless oceans

I did all my best to smile

'Til your singing eyes and fingers

Drew me loving to your isle

And you sang

Sail to me

Let me enfold you

Here I am

Waiting to hold you' - Song to The Siren, Tim Buckley (recording from 1970)

Tim was a psychedelic folk musician with a guitar sound that I've grown to love - there's an otherworldliness to the tone he maintains. Calling him a folk musician is perhaps a disservice to him - he has experimented with jazz, psychedelia and funk on various albums. Tim's life was short and brilliant - he died at 28 from a heroin overdose, but by then he had released nine studio albums, a lot of which were immense works of love and genius that stand the test of time.
