Update to let everyone know that one of my favorite musicians ever (Liz Harris aka Grouper) is releasing a new album 'Grid of Points' on April 27th! The self-titled track is already on bandcamp for listening too. Very excited for this one. Among her past works, Grouper's album 'Dragging a Dead Deer Up A Hill' is for me, a spiritual experience. It takes minimalistic chord progressions and pairs them with excellent ambient production. There's an element of sadness but more so of comfort... Or perhaps finding comfort in melancholy. The lyrics are often obfuscated and despite having heard the album n times I still don't know the lyrics to most songs (there are none online either so good luck with that). It melds elements of folk, ambient and psychedelia. It's the album that I heard first and went 'How does she do that?!?'. It's my go to brain fog album and clears away all neurotic tendencies (temporarily but effectively :p). The way I describe it doesn't really do justice to it, because musically it's just top notch, so many elements and you discover something new each time you hear it.
