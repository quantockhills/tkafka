'A heart that's full up like a landfill

A job that slowly kills you

Bruises that won't heal

You look so tired, unhappy

Bring down the government

They don't, they don't speak for us' - No Surprises, Radiohead

The quietness of the music and the lyrics really speaks to me. Radiohead are one of those artists that believe in 'less is more', and that often shows in their minimalistic approach to music. The lyrics often involve scattered phrases that complement each other to create a mood, as compared to a traditional songwriting narrative where lyrics have an order and often send a message. This video is from an acoustic concert in Paris, back in 2003. The collective gasp at the starting of the music is just wonderful... It truly would've been a joy to witness this live.
