Deerhunter Appreciation Post

"There's nothing I do in my life that's not stream of conscious. There's never been an interview where I didn't use that phrase at least 25 times, because people always want to know why I do things. They always want to know the story behind something, and it's like, I don't know. When I do laundry I do it stream of conscious. I don't even know what I'm doing, I just pick up random objects. Sometimes I wash candles accidentally." -Bradford Cox, Deerhunter
