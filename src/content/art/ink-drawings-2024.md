Last year I filled three A5 notebooks with ink drawings. Most were done in cafés, backstage at gigs, or on trains. Something about the permanence of ink — no erasing — forces a different kind of attention.

## Recurring Subjects

- **Machinery** — gears, pulleys, absurd kinetic contraptions
- **Portraits** — people on public transport, mostly from behind or in profile
- **Abstract patterns** — the kind that emerge when you stop thinking and let the pen wander
